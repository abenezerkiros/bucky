Frequently Asked Question

  • How do Generac generators work during a power outage?

    Generac standby generators automatically detect power loss and turn on within seconds to restore electricity. Once grid power returns, they automatically shut off and return to standby mode.

  • How often do Generac generators need maintenance?

    We recommend maintenance every 6-12 months depending on usage. We offer ongoing maintenance plans to keep your generator running smoothly year-round.

  • Can I monitor my generator remotely?

    Yes. With the Mobile Link™ app, you can track your generator’s status, receive alerts, and monitor maintenance needs from anywhere using your smartphone or tablet.

  • What size generator do I need?

    It depends on what you want to power during an outage. Our team provides free on-site assessments to calculate your home’s energy needs and recommend the right model.
